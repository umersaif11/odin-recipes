Karachi Biryani

Description:

Mutton/Goat Karahi (Karahi Gosht) is goat meat wrapped around a tomato-based curry,

However, you should allow the seasonings and spices to mix and coalesce in order to bring an authentic flavor to your table.

Ingredients

  • 2.5 lbs lamb or mutton on the bone cut in pieces.
  • 2 tbsp ginger and garlic paste.
  • 1 red onion chopped
  • 1 1/2 tsp cumin powder
  • 4-5 tomatoes chopped
  • 2 green chillies slit lengthwise

Steps

  1. In a large pot, add oil and fry onion until translucent.
  2. Next, add the meat and fry until no longer pink, about 6-8 minutes.
  3. Next, add in the ground spices including salt and mix for 4-5 minutes.
  4. At this point, the oil should have separated. Add in the green chilli and the garnishes and let simmer covered on low heat for another 25-30 mins.
  5. Serve hot with fresh tandoori naan.
