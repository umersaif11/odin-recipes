Karachi Biryani

Description:

Simply put, biryani is a spiced mix of meat and rice, traditionally cooked over an open fire in a leather pot. It is combined in different ways with a variety of components to create a number of highly tasty and unique flavor combinations.

Ingredients

  • Fennel seeds
  • Cinnamon
  • Bay leaves
  • Chicken
  • Rice

Steps

  1. Put the boiling water in a saucepan.
  2. Add the washed rice, boil for 5 minutes and drain.
  3. Layer the chicken mixture with the rice starting with the rice then the chicken mixture and repeat this twice
  4. Garnish with cumin seeds, and ginger.
  5. Lower the heat and simmer for a further 10 minutes.
